Apply for discount on business software

Eligible businesses can save 50% off the cost of one approved business software product, such as accounting and customer relationship management (CRM), up to a maximum of Â£5,000 (not including VAT).

To be eligible for the discount your business should:

  • be a company registered in the UK at Companies House or be a registered society listed on the Financial Conduct Authority (FCA) Mutuals Public Register
  • have been trading for more than 12 months before applying for the discount
  • employ between 5 and 249 people
  • be buying the software product for the first time

Upgrades, renewed subscriptions or add-on components to software you already own are not eligible for discount.

The person applying for the discount should be:

  • aged 18 or over
  • an employee of the business
  • authorised to agree to the terms and conditions on behalf of the business

What you'll need to tell us

You'll need to tell us:

  • the Companies House or FCA Mutuals Public Register number
  • your name, position in the company and work email address
